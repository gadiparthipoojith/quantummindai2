# AetherForge Collective — Brand Identity

## Studio Overview

**AetherForge Collective** is a premium AI engineering and data intelligence studio founded by **Gadiparthi Poojith** and **Surya Antarvedi**. We design, build, and deploy production-grade AI applications, intelligent agents, automation systems, and full-stack digital products for startups and enterprises.

---

## Logo Concept

**Mark:** An abstract **forge anvil** fused with a **neural constellation** — three interconnected nodes forming an upward arrow (growth) inside a hexagonal shield (engineering precision).

**Symbolism:**
- Hexagon → structure, reliability, enterprise trust
- Neural nodes → AI intelligence, agents, RAG
- Upward arrow → business growth, measurable outcomes
- Gradient mesh fill → creative AI studio energy

**Wordmark:** `AetherForge` in Geist Sans SemiBold + `Collective` in Geist Mono Regular, letter-spaced.

**Icon-only:** Hexagonal mark with violet→cyan gradient, usable at 16px favicon scale.
